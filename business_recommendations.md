# Business Recommendations

# Executive Summary

The analysis identifies **clear churn risks, retention opportunities, and revenue levers** across cities and customer segments.  
The recommendations below are designed to be **directly actionable** for marketplace growth teams and are monitored through a real-time Power BI dashboard.



## 1. RFM-Based Retention Strategy

- High-RFM customers show significantly higher retention compared to low-RFM groups

- Low-RFM customers account for a disproportionate share of churned customers

**Recommendation**

- Reward high-RFM users through loyalty benefits and exclusive offers

- Deploy reactivation campaigns for low-RFM users using targeted discounts and reminders



## 2. City-Specific Interventions

- Churn rates vary materially across cities, with Lahore and Karachi exhibiting higher churn levels

- Peshawar demonstrates comparatively stringer customer retention

**Recommendation**

- Allocate higher retention budgets and targeted campaigns to high-churn cities

- Replicate operational and engagement best practices from high-retention cities



## 3. Delivery & Rating Optimization

- Delivery experiences shows a strong relationship with customer churn

- A majority of orders fall into delayed or cancelled delivery states, driving disengagement

- Even well-rated orders experience churn when delivery delays occur

**Recommendation**

- Prioritise delivery SLAs for high-value and retained customers

- Treat delivery delays and cancellations as a leading churn risk



## 4. Restaurant Partnerships

- A limited set of restaurants contributes a disproportionate share of totao platform revenue

- Restaurant performance varies significantly across cities and partners
  
**Recommendation**

- Strengthen partnerships with top-performing restaurants to protect revenue concentration

- Introduce performance scorecards and improvement plans for underperforming partners



## 5. Revenue Growth Levers

- High-AOV orders contribute significantly to total revenue

- Retained customers generate a disproportionate share of revenue compared to churned users

**Recommendation**

- Promote bundles, subscriptions, and cross-sell offers for retained customers

- Personalise pricing and promotions using RFM-based segmentation
