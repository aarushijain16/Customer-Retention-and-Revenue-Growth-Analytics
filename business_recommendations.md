# Business Recommendations

# Executive Summary

The analysis identifies **clear churn risks, retention opportunities, and revenue levers** across cities and customer segments.  
The recommendations below are designed to be **directly actionable** for marketplace growth teams.



## 1. RFM-Based Retention Strategy

- High-RFM customers show near-zero churn

- Low-RFM customers account for the majority of churn

**Recommendation**

- Reward high-RFM users through loyalty benefits

- Deploy reactivation campaigns for low-RFM users using targeted discounts and reminders



## 2. City-Specific Interventions

- Lahore and Karachi show higher churn rates

- Peshawar demonstrates the strongest retention

**Recommendation**

- Allocate higher retention budgets to high-churn cities

- Replicate operational best practices from high-retention cities



## 3. Delivery & Rating Optimization

- Poor delivery experience strongly correlates with churn

- High-rated orders still churn when delivery delays occur

**Recommendation**

- Prioritise delivery SLAs for high-value customers

- Treat delivery failures as a leading churn indicator



## 4. Restaurant Partnerships

- A small subset of restaurants drives a disproportionate share of revenue

- Performance varies significantly across partners

**Recommendation**

- Strengthen partnerships with top-performing restaurants

- Use performance scorecards for underperforming partners



## 5. Revenue Growth Levers

- High-AOV categories offer strong upsell potential

- Repeat customers generate disproportionate revenue

**Recommendation**

- Promote bundles and subscriptions for repeat users

- Personalise offers using RFM-based segmentation
